I. At the drugstore

a) Dialogue

IV. I have a head ache.

If you feel some pain, you can tell about it with expression "У меня болит ..." [oo minya balit] indicating the location (part of the body or the organ) of the pain. In case the noun is in plural (e.g. eyes or teeth), it necessary to use "болят" [balyat] instead of "болит". Look at the following examples:

a) Look, listen and reapeat the examples:

У меня болит голова.
[oo minya balit galava]    

I have a headache.

У меня болит живот.
[oo minya balit jivot]    

I have a stomach-ache.

У меня болят глаза.
[oo minya balyat glaza]    

My eyes heart me.
